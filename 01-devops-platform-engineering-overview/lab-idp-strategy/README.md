# Lab: IDP Strategy — Platform Engineering Consultant

Take the role of a **platform engineering consultant** helping **NovaStream**, a fast-growing startup, design their first internal developer platform (IDP) — with **TaskFlow** as the coordination layer for platform initiatives.

## Scenario

```bash
cat scenario/NOVASTREAM.md
```

NovaStream is scaling from 20 to 80 engineers. Leadership wants self-service without losing control. Your job is strategic design, not tool installation.

## Deliverables

Complete `deliverables/` using `templates/` as guides:

| File | Focus |
|------|--------|
| `DEVELOPER_EXPERIENCE_ASSESSMENT.md` | Pain points, golden paths, DX gaps |
| `PLATFORM_CAPABILITIES.md` | What the IDP must provide (and defer) |
| `TEAM_STRUCTURE.md` | Platform team model, interfaces with product teams |
| `PLATFORM_ROADMAP.md` | Phased plan aligned to business goals |

Reference solutions are in `solutions/` — attempt your own analysis first.
